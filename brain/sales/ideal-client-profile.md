# Ideal Client Profile

## Demographics

Established practitioners in:
- Business coaching
- Executive consulting
- Therapy (clinical)
- Education and training
- Specialist consulting (B2B and B2C)

Deployed successfully in 50+ countries across dozens of industries.

## Psychographics

- Has a track record of real client results (not theoretical)
- Revenue ceiling feels tied to personal hours and energy
- Prospects say "I need to think about it" after sales calls
- Uses vague language on calls: "holistic approach," "depends on the client"
- Methodology changes slightly every time they explain it
- Undercharging relative to transformation delivered

## Buying Signals

- Asks "how exactly does this work?" on calls and struggles to answer clearly
- Wants to scale but fears burnout from more clients
- Interested in courses, books, or group programs but doesn't know where to start
- Has been coaching/consulting 3+ years with consistent results

## Disqualification Red Flags

- No paying clients yet
- Cannot articulate who they help or what results they deliver
- Looking for a funnel template without real expertise behind it
- Wants to keep methodology entirely mysterious and custom per client
- Not willing to codify their method into a named framework

Free 30-minute call. Strictly for established practitioners. No pitch, no pressure — clear roadmap only.
