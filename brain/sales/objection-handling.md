# Objection Handling

## "I need to think about it"

**Root cause:** Prospect rejected ambiguity, not talent. They could not see the specific path from pain to outcome.

**Response angle:** The issue is not your ability — it is that your expertise is invisible. The C.O.A.C.H.E.S. Curve makes your method visible so prospects buy certainty, not improvisation.

## "Will this work for my niche?"

**Response:** Framework packaging principles are industry-agnostic. Deployed in 50+ countries across B2B consulting, clinical health, executive coaching. The structural shift from expert labor to asset owner is the same regardless of niche.

## "How much of my time is required?"

**Concierge:** ~3 hours across 3 calls. Team handles heavy lifting.
**Accelerator:** More client time — you execute with strategic guidance.

## "Why not just use templates?"

**Response:** Templates accelerate execution but cannot create differentiated authority. The revenue cap is rarely lack of web pages — it is marketing an unstructured, vague offer. Scale only happens when methodology is clarified first.

## "Is the equity partnership guaranteed?"

**Response:** No — it is performance-based. Complete program, build assets, demonstrate market traction. Concierge clients receive priority consideration.

## "How does the AI automation work?"

**Response:** Specific AI tools and automated workflows handle routine research, copy generation, and client onboarding. Proven to reduce manual workload by 80-90%. This orchestrator prototype demonstrates that capability.
