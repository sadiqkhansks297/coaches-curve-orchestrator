# C.O.A.C.H.E.S. Curve Overview

The operating system that converts private, trapped expertise into saleable IP, scalable delivery, revenue architecture, and market authority.

## The Core Problem: Non-Transferable Expertise

Established experts do not have a lead generation problem, a pricing problem, or a talent problem. They have a **non-transferable expertise problem**.

If your process lives only in your head, every sale depends on your ability to perform clarity in real time. You do not own a business asset; you own a high-maintenance explanation.

## Who This Is For

Established coaches, consultants, therapists, educators, and specialists who:
- Have proven they can help people with real results
- Are tired of being the primary bottleneck in their business
- Feel revenue depends too much on custom delivery and founder energy
- Know they are undercharging relative to transformations they facilitate
- Want to stop selling manual labor and start building premium IP

## Who This Is NOT For

- Beginners still searching for first paying clients
- People who have not validated an offer or achieved real results
- Those looking for a generic "magic funnel" to mask low-quality service
- Experts who insist on keeping methodology custom, mysterious, and chaotic

## The Five Steps

1. **Positioning** — Define who you help, what they want, how they describe the problem
2. **Asset Innovation** — Turn method into visible assets: framework, book, course, workshop deck
3. **Revenue Architecture** — Connect framework to buyer journey: webinar, offer sheet, sales system
4. **Operations** — Onboarding SOPs, AI automation stack, reduce founder dependency by 80-90%
5. **Authority** — Speaking & media kit, podcast strategy, 90-day visibility plan

## Tagline

From Expert Labor To Asset Owner — escape the time-for-money trap.
