# Strategy Session Prep SOP

## Purpose

Prepare Mohammed Ali for a free 30-minute strategy session with a qualified prospect.

## Pre-Call Checklist

1. **Qualify** — Confirm prospect is established expert (not beginner)
2. **Research** — LinkedIn, website, content presence, niche
3. **Map to C.O.A.C.H.E.S. step** — Which of the 5 steps are they stuck on?
4. **Identify bottleneck** — Expert labor trap? Vague positioning? No assets? No systems?
5. **Recommend offer** — Accelerator (DIY capacity) vs Concierge (wants DFY)
6. **Prepare 3 discovery questions** tailored to their situation

## Discovery Questions (Default)

1. "When a prospect asks how your method works, can you explain it the same way every time?"
2. "What happens to your pipeline when you are not available to explain your value on a call?"
3. "If you could productize one piece of your expertise this month, what would it be?"

## Call Structure

- Review their current business model (5 min)
- Evaluate raw material of their expertise (10 min)
- Determine where they sit on the Curve today (10 min)
- Clear roadmap — no pitch, no pressure (5 min)

## Post-Call

- Log prospect in knowledge base
- Note C.O.A.C.H.E.S. step, recommended offer, follow-up actions
- Draft personalized follow-up within 24 hours

## Red Flags to Note

- Cannot describe ideal client
- No validated offer or results
- Wants "magic funnel" without expertise
- Resistant to codifying methodology
